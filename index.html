Anna Zakharenko

English and Russian teacher, founder of English as a way to grow

I’ve been giving one-to-one English lessons since 2011. In 2017 I started teaching Russian. I specialise in working with beginners who wish to speak Russian / English at a native-like level. I love helping students learn to pronounce sounds and make sentences using various grammar structures. My ultimate goal as a teacher is to help my students become independent non-native Russian / English speakers. Apart from giving one-to-one lessons, I help personal development fans learn to understand podcasts in English so they can improve their life on the go.
